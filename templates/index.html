ABOUT

Our Mission

Our Mission is to preach good tidings
unto the meek. To proclam liberty to the
captive, to comfort all that mourn, to bind
up the brokenhearted. To spread the the joy
and happiness that comes from the guidance
of the Light of the world, Jesus Christ.

Founder and G.O

The Founder and General Overseer of
Light of the world Evangelical Ministry
is Jesus Christ for he alone is the Light of
the world
